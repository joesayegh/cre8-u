About

Hi, I'm Cynthia Paim Cohen. I'm a wife and mother of 3, originally from Brazil. I have passion for fitness & wellbeing and for helping my clients deliver life changing results. I understand how hard it can be to start or get back into an exercise routine…. Been there, done that!

I specialise in Women’s Fitness with an emphasis on program design for strength and conditioning training, weight management and core strength & stability.

In the end it is all about prioritising and making a commitment with yourself to be the best that you can be.

Most importantly, I will be there to support you step by step through your journey to make sure you deliver on your goals.

Please feel free to get in contact.

Services

Personal Training

Benefit from dedicated one-to-one sessions specifically tailored to help you to achieve your health and fitness goals. Your personal programme will be designed to start from where you are physically right now and will not only be progressive and challenging but also fun so you are always engaged.

Women's Specialist

I have a passion for helping women feel better about themselves and to help them feel happier about their bodies. Age is just a number, so whether you are in your teens, pre or post-natal, in your 30s, 40s, 50’s or even over 60 I am here to help you become the strongest and healthiest version of yourself.

Nutritional Adviser

I can analyse your current habits and nutritional intake to recommend changes that will benefit you inside and out, physically and mentally all based on scientific facts not fads. I want to help you make changes that will last you for the rest of your life, not just the next few weeks. This is more than fitness and diet, this is about wellbeing.

Animal Flow

Animal Flow is ground based movement, made fun, challenging and effective. This system is designed to improve strength, power, flexibility, mobility, and coordination for all levels of fitness enthusiasts. Train with a professional. Animal Flow has something for every body! You don’t have to be super mobile or super strong to do it.
