Personal Training

Benefit from dedicated one-to-one sessions specifically tailored to help you to achieve your full health and fitness development goals.

Of course you are unique and have different abilities and goals to anyone else. Your personal programme will be designed to start from where you are now and will be progressive, challenging and fun. Your current physical abilities and parameters will be taken as the starting point and the achievement of your goals will be the finishing line.

Whether you are looking to lose weight, build lean muscle, get stronger or be more flexible, agile and stable it is all achievable if you are dedicated.

It is also important to understand the impact that your diet has on your performance and your health and I can advise you based on scientific facts not fads.
